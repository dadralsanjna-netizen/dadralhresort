Our Royal Legacy

Originally built in 1923 as the private residence of the noble Thakur family, Dadralh Resort stands as a magnificent example of Indo-Saracenic architecture. This heritage property has been meticulously restored to preserve its original grandeur while offering modern comforts.

The resort features stunning courtyards with traditional jharokhas (overhanging balconies), intricate frescoes depicting Rajasthani folklore, and hand-carved marble pillars. Each of our 45 rooms and suites is uniquely decorated with antique furnishings that reflect the royal heritage of Rajasthan.
